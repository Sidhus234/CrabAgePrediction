This folder contains a csv file with data for the project (both input and output)

Key variables in the dataset are:
  ------------ Independent Variables --------------------------
  1. Sex: Char variable (M/F). Describes the gender of the Crab 
  2. Length: of the Crab
  3. Diameter: of the Crab
  4. Height: of the Crab
  5. Weight: of the Crab
  6. Shucked Weight: of the Crab
  7. Viscera Weight: of the Crab
  8. Shell Weight: of the Crab
  ---------------- Dependent Variable ------------------
  9. Age: Age of the Crab (Dependent Variable)
